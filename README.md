# Hi, I'm Vidisha. 👋

I think in strategy. I'm learning to build.

### A bit about me

I'm an economist-strategist currently finishing my **MSc in Economics & Strategy at Imperial College Business School** (graduating August 2026). Before that, I spent three years as a strategist at **DDB Mudra Group (Omnicom)** in Mumbai, where I led strategy on accounts like McDonald's India, IKEA, and Audi.

Right now I'm interning at **Avegen**, a London healthtech startup, working directly with a co-founder on commercial strategy, a digital therapeutics distribution partnership with King's College London, and building internal automation tools from scratch.

I also founded a community lending library at age 14 that grew to 1,300+ books. All revenue went to a local orphanage. That was probably my first real product.

---

### Why I'm here

I'm not a developer by training. I'm a founder in progress.

I started building because I got tired of having ideas I couldn't prototype myself. I use Claude Code and VS Code to turn briefs into working products. The goal isn't to become an engineer — it's to close the gap between *thinking* something and *making* something.

My long-term focus is building a venture in India, somewhere at the intersection of finance, informal economy, and middle-income access.
